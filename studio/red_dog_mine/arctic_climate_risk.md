## 1 · The validated insight (the gated read — single source of truth)

**The full grounded read is `../../docs/extra/reddog_bottom_up_report_2026-06.md`** (§1–§7, every claim sourced + dated + fact/projection-labelled; the evidence record is `../../docs/discussion/2026-06-14_outlier_method_red_dog.md`). This §1 is its gate-record; nothing in the dressing below exceeds it.

**Claim.** Red Dog is a **single-point-of-failure energy-and-logistics asset in the fastest-warming region on Earth**: one ~100-day ice-free window (≈ Jul–Oct) carries **both** the year's diesel **in** (~15.5M gal) and the entire concentrate export **out**, so a short/late/stormy season threatens fuel security *and* revenue timing at once — already proven (Q4-2025 Red Dog gross profit ~$200M vs ~$303M a year earlier, on shipping delays). Layered on it: a permafrost water-balance crisis a US regulator has **formally conceded is unfixable** (ADEC's 10-yr-to-~2036 schedule; "no reasonably available remedy to the thawing of permafrost"), pushing a **~$279M adaptation capex onto a mine with ~6 years of ore left**, an escalating royalty, and an owner mid-merger into a copper major. The tail risk — a diesel-tank or tailings-dam failure on thawing ground — is the **realized Norilsk-2020 geometry (~$2B)**.

**Scope**: the asset (off-grid ~25 MW diesel · 1 road · 1 shallow Chukchi port) + the Arctic-industrial class it instantiates. **Off-substrate** — no InfraSure plant/ISO/MCP record exists.
